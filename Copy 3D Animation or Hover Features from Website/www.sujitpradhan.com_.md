1:1 Mentorship

My portfolio is messy (or non-existent). Should I wait to book?

Not at all. In fact, early-stage chaos is often the best time to talk. We don’t need a polished website to have a breakthrough. We can review raw Figma files, discuss your storytelling structure, or work on your personal positioning. I’d rather help you build the right foundation than critique a polished mistake.

I’m switching from Engineering (or another field) to Design. Can you help?

100%. I made that exact jump myself. I know the struggle of shifting from 'logic' to 'empathy.' I can help you translate your technical background into a unique superpower, showing recruiters why your diverse experience is an asset, not a liability.

What is your feedback style? Will you be nice?

I’m kind, but I’m not here to just pat you on the back. I believe in 'Radical Candor.' I won’t sugarcoat things because that doesn’t help you grow. Expect honest, direct, and constructive critique designed to push your work to a Senior or Big Tech standard.

How can I make sure I don't waste our time?

Come with a specific goal. 'Look at my portfolio' is okay, but 'I'm struggling to explain the problem statement in this project' is better. If you have links or specific questions, drop them in the booking note so I can prep beforehand. The more context you give me, the more value you get.
